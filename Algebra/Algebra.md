
Given a [[Field]] $k$ (or commutative [[Ring]]), a *$k$-algebra* $A$ is a $k$-[[Vector Space]] (or $R$-[[Module]]) with a product $\cdot: A\times A \rightarrow A$ s.t. the following holds for $x,y\in A$ and $\lambda \in k$

* $(x+y)z = xz+yz$
* $x(y+z)=xy+xz$
* $\lambda (xy) = (\lambda x)y$
